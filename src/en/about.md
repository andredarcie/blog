# André N. Darcie

Software developer and computer scientist, focused on backend web development and game creation. Passionate about the fusion between humanities and exact sciences, between art and technology.

## Career

Senior developer at **NTT Data**, assigned to **XP Investimentos**, where I work in the credit card benefits area. Previously worked at the startup **Exato Digital**, on projects for clients such as Grupo Globo, Quinto Andar, B3, Stone, and Huawei.

Bachelor's in Computer Science and postgraduate in Applied Data Science from **PUC Minas**, with three years of scientific research supported by CNPq.

Open-source contributor with notable projects on GitHub: **C# Quick Reference Guide**, **Pragmatic ASP.NET Course**, and **Awesome GTFS**.

## Other projects

- **OpenVideoGameData**: co-founded with Diego Penha, a nonprofit initiative for open availability of game data. Reached ~500 thousand views on Reddit.
- **Book Club in the Mountains**: organizer and founder, a club focused on collective reading of technical and scientific books.
- Indie game developer. Notable titles: *Brian's Mansion*, *Insomnia*, *Life is Undefined*, *Get Out*, *Zero to One*, and *Zero The Hero*.
- Virtual and mixed reality enthusiast, with videos that reached millions of views on TikTok.
- Digital nomad sharing experiences and discoveries around the world.
- Professor and consultant specializing in C# and .NET.
